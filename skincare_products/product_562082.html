    What it is: A two-in-one face scrub and mask that buffs and hydrates with real brown sugar and strawberries.

    Highlighted Ingredients:
    - Brown Sugar: A natural humectant that seals in moisture, gently buffs away dry, dull skin.
    - Real Wild Strawberries: Packed with vitamin C to help to brighten and soothe.

    What Else You Need to Know: Looking for an exfoliator that isn't harsh or drying? This is it.
