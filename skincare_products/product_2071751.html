    What it is:
    A brightening, vitamin-C-rich gel cream that hydrates for up to 24 hours and diminishes the look of fine lines and wrinkles.

    Solutions for:
    - Dullness and uneven texture
    - Dryness
    - Fine lines and wrinkles

    If you want to know more…
    This innovative, peach-tinted gel cream instantly illuminates and delivers an energizing burst of hydration. It targets fine lines and wrinkles, renews youthful radiance, and visibly firms skinbut that’s not all. It even improves makeup application, creating a smooth, hydrated canvas for foundation and concealer.

    it’s powered by a unique combination of three sources of vitamin C, chosen specifically for their efficacy in skincare: ascorbic acid, which is pure vitamin C; and 3-O-ethyl ascorbic acid and tetrahexyldecyl ascorbate, two light-and-air stable vitamin C derivatives known for their antioxidant properties.

    What else you need to know:
    Ole Henriksen is always looking for the most innovative textures and efficacious ingredients to create unmatched sensorial experiences, and this ultra-light gel creaminspired by bursts of freshly squeezed juiceis no exception. Even with its weightless texture, it delivers the lasting hydration your skin craves.

    Research results:
    In an independent, clinical study on 35 women using C-Rush Brightening Gel Crème, all users experienced:
    - 140% increase in hydration

    In an independent, clinical study on 35 women using C-Rush Brightening Gel Crème over 4 weeks:
    - 97% of users experienced hydration for up to 24 hours
    - 60% average increase in skin’s firmness according to all users

    Sources of vitamin C:
    -Ascorbic Acid: Pure vitamin C, Ole’s "gold standard"e; for addressing visible signs of aging.
    -3-O-Ethyl Ascorbic Acid: A form of vitamin C that is proven to be stable against oxidation and light degradation; helps maintain stability of ascorbic acid.
    -Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate: A stable form of vitamin C that is known for its antioxidant benefits.
    -Rose of Winter: Rich in antioxidants to help nourish and condition skin.

    Suggested Usage:

    -Apply evenly to face and neck in the AM and PM after OLEHENRIKSEN Truth Serum® (sold separately).

    -Gently massage in an upward, circular motion.

    -For external use only.

    -Use as directed.

    -Avoid eye area.
